Integer
- whole numbers - 100, 1000, 0

- Byte
	- 1 byte (8 bits)
	- first bit is for sign - or +
	- remaining 7 bits 
	- 2^7 = 128 
	value from -2^7 to +(2^7-1)
	-128 to 0 to 127
- Short
	- 2 byte (16 bits)
	value from -2^15 to (2^15 -1)
- Integer
	- 4 byte (32 bits)
	-2^31 to (2^31-1)
- Long
	- 8 byte (64 bits)
	-2^63 to (2^63-1)
	
Decimals
- Float 
	- 4 byte
- Double
	- 8 byte
	
Character
- 2 byte
